95 /*
96  * Combine a shape's material with the material of the shape's geometry.
97  * The shape's material is written during cga generation. It defaults to "CityEngineMaterial".
98  * As long as it isn't changed, it is overridden by it's geometry's material (e.g. if you insert [i()]

104  * It is not trivial, use this function.
