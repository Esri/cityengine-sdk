const prtx::MaterialPtrVector& prtx::EncodePreparator::FinalizedInstance::getMaterials ( ) const

Returns the finalized materials corresponding to the meshes in the geometry returned by getGeometry(). Note that in the case of enabled instancing, these are the final materials as specified in the rules, whereas the materials attached to the meshes are the original asset materials. There is one material for each mesh in the geometry.
